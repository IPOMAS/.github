# IPOMAS: Integrated Intelligent Palm Oil Mill Automated Solution

## Description

IPOMAS is an intelligent industrial ecosystem designed to modernize and optimize palm oil mill operations through automation, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Industrial IoT, and intelligent monitoring systems.

The platform integrates operational management, predictive analytics, real-time monitoring, AI-powered decision support, and smart automation into a unified digital infrastructure.

Our mission is to improve operational efficiency, reduce downtime, enhance production quality, support predictive maintenance, and enable data-driven decision making for modern palm oil industries.
